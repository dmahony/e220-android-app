## Design Context

### Users
Primary audience: private hacker chat users. They are using the app as a real chat surface for Bluetooth-to-radio messaging rather than as a config utility. The app should prioritize conversation flow, message readability, and efficient use of limited mobile screen space.

### Brand Personality
Signal-inspired, private, focused, efficient, understated.
Emotional goals: trustworthy, calm, compact, intentional.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use both light and dark themes. The visual target is Signal for Android: clean conversation-first layout, restrained chrome, strong message hierarchy, and polished but not flashy surfaces. Avoid bulky admin/dashboard UI and avoid wasting vertical space.

### Design Principles
1. Chat first, always: the conversation should dominate the screen and config/debug affordances must stay secondary.
2. Use space tightly and deliberately: compact status treatment, efficient composer layout, and no oversized cards or dead air.
3. Privacy over ornament: polished, quiet surfaces and high clarity instead of decorative effects.
4. Signal-like clarity: message bubbles, typography, and composer interactions should feel familiar, stable, and production-grade.
5. Light and dark parity: both themes should feel intentional rather than one being an afterthought.
